Campinas Mediation Center of the Chamber of Conciliation, Mediation and Arbitration Ciesp/Fiesp (“CCMA Ciesp/Fiesp”)

The Campinas Mediation Center is a branch of CCMA Ciesp/Fiesp. The Center promotes the use and facilitates the recourse to meditation and of the other Adequate Dispute Resolution Methods (ADRs) by local businesspeople.

Such tools help companies reduce the costs incurred in managing disputes with business partners, and contribute to healthier business relations and promote peace within the community.

The Campinas Branch of the Center of the Industries of the State of São Paulo is ready to receive requests for mediation and to conduct locally the mediation sessions. The mediation proceedings will be administered by CCMA Ciesp/Fiesp in accordance with its Mediation Rules. Hence, the Campinas Mediation Center benefits since its inception of the expertise developed by CCMA Ciesp/Fiesp during its 23 years of practice.

If a request for mediation is received by the Ciesp in Campinas, the mediation request is relayed to the Secretariat of the CCMA Ciesp/Fiesp in São Paulo, and the latter oversees the proceedings, even if all acts and meetings take place at the Ciesp in Campinas.

The Campinas Mediation Center offers a specific Mediator’s roster, with specialists located closer to the users and who know the peculiars of the economy of the region. The Center is capable of hosting hearings and meetings at its offices in Campinas, allowing the parties to conduct the mediation proceedings without having to travel to São Paulo.

In addition to promoting dispute resolution services, the Campinas Mediation Center promotes meetings, lectures and seminars in order to promote the culture of mediating conflicts.
